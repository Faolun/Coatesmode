Old Man LAN serves as one of the last bastions of old school communal gaming experiences. Relive the days of gaming with wreckless abandon and stuffing your face full of garbage. Bask in the glory of limitless gaming. Initiated in 2015, we serve to reignite the fires created during the golden era of gaming, bringing together those with a shared love for classic titles that have left an indelible mark on your gaming DNA. The event is a haven for those seeking to immerse themselves in the active physical recreation. From the iconic LAN games of the '90s and early 2000s LAN party staples to the modern Boomeresque titles, Old Man LAN exists to allow you to relive the pixelated fever dreams that fueled your passions in the first place. The atmosphere is electric, charged with an virulent blend of excitement and nostalgia as you and your fellow gamers share stories and recount the thrill of combatting friends without an internet connection. Old Man LAN isn't just a gathering; it's a pilgrimage for those who appreciate the communal spirit that defined the early days of LAN gaming. In a culture constantly racing towards another video game collapse, Old Man LAN stands as a testament to the cornerstones, long paved over, that hold the pillars of gaming culture.

Directions

1) Address:

17 Prince Edward Road, Great Meadows, NJ 07838

2) Unloading:

Pull up to the furthest garage door and deposit your belongings in the garage staging area. Do not use the front door, the bluestone path is slippery in winter!

3)Parking:

Do not block the driveway so people can load and unload. If weather permits and the ground is not muddy. Park perpendicular to the driveway on the lawn opposite of the house (yellow area). Pull up to the hedges to leave room for people to park behind you if possible, especially if you are spending the night. If weather is not permitting, parking will have to be solely on the street. Please do not block mailboxes. If it snows, please carpool, if possible, as parking may be taken up by plow walls.
